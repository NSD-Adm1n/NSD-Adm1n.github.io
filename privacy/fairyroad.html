Privacy Policy for Fairy Road: Endless Runner

Last Updated: 15th July 2025

Thank you for downloading and playing Fairy Road, a free game by Never Say Die Studios Pvt Ltd. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information is handled by our game.

1. No Personal Data Collection by Us

We, Never Say Die Studios, do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from or about our users. Our game does not include features for creating user accounts, and we do not ask you for your name, email, physical address, or any other personal details.

2. Locally Stored Game Data

The game saves your progress, such as high scores, gameplay statistics and settings preferences (e.g., volume levels), directly on your device. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. It can be cleared or deleted by clearing the app's cache or data, or by uninstalling the game from your device.

3. Third-Party Services: Unity Engine

Our game is developed using the Unity game engine. Unity Technologies may collect some non-personal, anonymous data for its own purposes, such as engine improvements, analytics, and diagnostics. This may include generic device information (e.g., device model, operating system version), and anonymized usage statistics (e.g., how long the game is played, features used).

We, Never Say Die Studios, do not have access to or control over the data Unity collects. We encourage you to review Unity's Privacy Policy to understand their data practices: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Children's Privacy

Our game, Fairy Road, is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children through our game. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly take appropriate action.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy will be revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
